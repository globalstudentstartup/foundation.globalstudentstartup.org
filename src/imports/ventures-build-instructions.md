Section 3 — The Model (4 Steps)
Heading: How It Works
4 sequential steps:
1. Global Scouting via GSSF Friends & Family
Trusted regional leaders, mentors, alumni, and advisors nominate standout teams. Open applications are limited and intentional. This distributed network serves as GSSF Ventures' global eyes and ears, enabling discovery beyond traditional startup pipelines.
2. Select Teams & Golden Tickets
Ten (10) Select Teams are chosen per cohort from the referred pool. Each team receives a Golden Ticket to the in-person bootcamp. Selection is based on quality, integrity, and founder potential — with no pay-to-play access.
3. Baselining Bootcamp (In-Person)
An intensive, in-person bootcamp designed to establish a baseline across traction, clarity, and execution; refine venture narratives and positioning; stress-test assumptions and business models; and prepare teams for investor-level conversations. The goal is not acceleration — it is credible signal creation.
4. Golden Ticket Pitch Sessions
Private, invitation-only pitch sessions with venture capital firms, accelerators, and strategic ecosystem partners. Focused on meaningful engagement, not public demo-day theatrics.

Section 7 — Funding Model
Heading: How It's Funded
Body text:
GSSF Ventures is supported through a mission-aligned sponsorship model. Sponsorships from VC firms, accelerators, and ecosystem partners fund Select Team travel and participation, in-person bootcamp programming, venture preparation, and investor access. GSSF Ventures does not take equity and does not charge teams for participation.
Sponsorship benchmark note: $25,000 USD per Select Team, pooled across one or more sponsors.
